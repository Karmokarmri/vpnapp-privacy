Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

No-logs VPN

This Privacy Policy explains how VPN App ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

We collect the minimum data necessary to provide the VPN service:

  • Account information — email address when you register (optional)
  • System language — used only to set the app language. Not stored.
  • IP address and country — used only to show you the nearest server. Not stored or logged.
  • Crash reports — anonymous crash data via Firebase Crashlytics to improve stability.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT log your VPN traffic
  • We do NOT log your browsing history
  • We do NOT log your DNS queries
  • We do NOT log connection timestamps
  • We do NOT sell your data to any third party
  • We do NOT store your IP address

No-Logs Policy

We strictly adhere to a no-logs policy. Your internet activity through the VPN is never monitored, recorded, stored, or shared with anyone. We cannot identify what you do while connected to our VPN.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication, crash reporting, remote configuration. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob — advertisements shown to free users. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing — in-app purchases for Premium.

Data Security

All VPN traffic is encrypted using WireGuard protocol. We use industry-standard security practices to protect any account information stored in our systems.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
